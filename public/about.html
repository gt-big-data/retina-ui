What is this?

RETINA

Real-Time Intelligent News App

Project

Similar to Google News, RETINA compiles top news sources and conveys the information into a web/mobile interface complete with news recommendations.

Main Components of the Project

Crawling -
Integrating all sources to links in HTML.
Clustering -
Organizes data based on titles and keywords.
API-
Returns news results based on recommendations
UI -
Responsible for the web/mobile app and account creation/registration.
